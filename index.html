Our Story

Not just a restaurant.
A place people remember.

96 RANCH was born from a simple belief: that the best meals are never eaten alone.

Every dish we serve carries the weight of shared tables, family memories, and the kind of food that makes you close your eyes for a second when you take that first bite.

We cook the way your lola would — with patience, with heart, and with the understanding that food isn't just fuel. It's how we show love.

"You don't just eat here.
You remember eating here."
