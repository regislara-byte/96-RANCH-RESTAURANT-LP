# 96 RANCH — LIQUID CUTTING BOARD POSTER V1

## Objective

Transform the iconic 96 Ranch cutting board logo into a luxury editorial poster using glossy liquid ink aesthetics while preserving brand recognition.

## Creative Direction

Do not create a restaurant advertisement.

Create a museum-quality visual identity poster.

The artwork should feel like a fusion of:

* Luxury fashion editorial
* Contemporary art gallery poster
* Premium hospitality branding
* Minimalist design exhibition

## Main Subject

The recognizable 96 Ranch cutting board logo.

The cutting board frame, spoon, fork, and handle should seamlessly emerge from glossy black liquid ink.

The liquid should form elegant sculptural drips, flowing organic shapes, reflective surfaces, and fluid contours.

The structure must remain recognizable as the 96 Ranch logo.

## Typography

Keep typography crisp and readable.

Do not melt the text.

Typography should feel premium and editorial.

* "96" in elegant luxury serif style
* "RANCH" in clean modern sans-serif style
* Strong spacing
* Sophisticated composition
* High-end magazine aesthetic

## Composition

* Centered vertical poster layout
* Vast white negative space
* Single dominant subject
* Balanced luxury composition
* Minimal distractions
* Gallery-quality presentation

## Visual Style

Glossy black liquid ink

Reflective liquid metal appearance

Organic flowing drips

High contrast black and white palette

Photorealistic details

Luxury editorial design

Museum-quality digital artwork

Sculptural fluid dynamics

Premium branding aesthetic

Contemporary minimalist poster

Ultra-clean background

Elegant sophistication

## Lighting

Soft studio lighting

Subtle dramatic reflections

Premium product photography quality

High detail surface reflections

Luxury visual depth

## Color Palette

Primary:

* Pure White
* Deep Gloss Black

Optional Accent:

* Very subtle metallic silver reflections

Avoid:

* Bright colors
* Neon effects
* Cartoon styling
* Overly aggressive dripping
* Graffiti aesthetics
