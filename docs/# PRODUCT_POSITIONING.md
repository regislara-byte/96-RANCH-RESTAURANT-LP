## Product Description

96 RANCH Restaurant LP is a premium restaurant landing page designed to transform visitors into customers through visual food storytelling, mobile-first engagement, and direct social media conversion.

Unlike traditional restaurant websites that only display menus and contact information, this landing page creates a food discovery experience where visitors browse dishes, explore promotions, discover customer favorites, and connect directly with the restaurant.

The concept combines:

* Netflix-style discovery
* Apple-inspired spacing
* Food craving psychology
* Social media engagement
* Family dining storytelling
