---

## Floating Brand Seal Rules

Movement:

* 2–5px maximum
* Slow
* Organic
* Subtle
* Inspired by Bossa Nova rhythm

Avoid:

* Bounce
* Spin
* Pulse
* Flashing glow
* Gaming-style effects

The logo should feel alive but calm.
