# IMPLEMENTATION_001.md

# 96 RANCH Restaurant LP — Craving-Driven Foundation

## Framework

Applied Engineering AI + Human Intelligence

## Project Goal

Create a mobile-first, story-driven restaurant landing page for 96 RANCH that makes visitors feel hungry, curious, and ready to act.

The page should not feel like a static menu.
It should feel like a food destination.

## Emotional Direction

96 RANCH is not only selling food.

It is selling:

* Craving
* Comfort
* Family time
* Celebration
* Barkada moments
* Local food memories

## Design DNA

* Apple clarity and spacing
* NVIDIA dark premium hierarchy
* Nike emotional storytelling
* Netflix-style food discovery
* Hershey-style craving psychology
* Facebook-first local restaurant flow

## Core Feeling

When someone opens the page, they should think:

“Why am I suddenly hungry?”

## Primary CTA

* Message on Facebook
* Call / Text
* Find Location
* View Menu

## Page Sections

### 1. Hero

Big emotional opening.

Suggested copy:

Some meals fill your stomach.
Some meals bring people together.

Your Ultimate Food Destination.

From family celebrations to everyday cravings, 96 RANCH serves flavors people remember.

CTA:

* View Menu
* Message Us
* Find Location

### 2. Trust Stats

Short confidence signals.

Examples:

* Local Food Destination
* Family-Friendly Dining
* Event-Ready Meals
* Customer Favorites

### 3. Today’s Cravings

Replace normal “Featured Dishes.”

Use large mouth-watering food cards.

Example:

* Lemon Butter Fish
* Firecracker Beef
* Caramel Chicken
* Sizzling Chicken Poppers
* Lechon Baka

### 4. Most Loved This Month

Netflix-style horizontal ranking.

Use:
#1
#2
#3
#4
#5

No bar graph.

This should feel like trending food, not analytics.

### 5. What Are You Craving?

Hershey-style category cards.

Suggested categories:

* Something Crispy
* Something Savory
* Something Hearty
* Family Favorites
* Celebration Platters
* Refreshing Drinks

### 6. Restaurant Story

Warm documentary tone.

Focus on:

* Local dining
* Family meals
* Shared tables
* Food memories
* Community presence

### 7. Family / Events

Position 96 RANCH as a place for:

* Birthdays
* Family gatherings
* Barkada meals
* Small celebrations
* Group dining
* Special occasions

### 8. Gallery

Use food + people.

Food creates hunger.
People create trust.
Together they create action.

### 9. Location

Simple section with:

* Location button
* Map placeholder
* Facebook link
* Call / text number

### 10. Facebook CTA

Main conversion section.

Suggested copy:

Craving something today?

Message 96 RANCH on Facebook and ask what’s available.

CTA:
Message on Facebook

### 11. Contact / Footer

Include:

* Restaurant name
* Facebook
* Call / Text
* Location
* Short tagline

## Visual Rules

* Dark premium background
* Red / warm accent inspired by 96 RANCH identity
* Big food cards
* Rounded corners
* Mobile-first spacing
* Sticky CTA on mobile
* Avoid clutter
* Avoid corporate dashboard feel

## Build Rule

Do not copy Netflix, Hershey, Apple, Nike, or NVIDIA directly.

Borrow only:

* Structure
* Spacing logic
* Emotional flow
* Card discovery pattern
* CTA clarity

## Asset Rule

Use available uploaded 96 RANCH visuals as reference only unless actual image files are placed in assets.

Recommended folders:

* assets/logo/
* assets/hero/
* assets/food/
* assets/gallery/
* assets/vla/
