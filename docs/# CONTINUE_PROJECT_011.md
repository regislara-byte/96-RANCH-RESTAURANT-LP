## Opportunities for Refinement

### 1. Floating Brand Seal

The floating logo currently competes with the hero.

Instead of acting as the focal point,

it should become the signature.

Recommendations:

* Reduce visual weight
* Slightly reduce size
* Position higher within the hero
* Allow surrounding content to breathe
* Preserve subtle floating movement

The seal should quietly reinforce the brand rather than dominate it.

---

### 2. Visual Hierarchy

Current hero contains several competing focal points.

The visitor's attention should naturally flow:

Brand

↓

Headline

↓

Food

↓

Call-to-Action

Improve spacing and hierarchy where appropriate.

---

### 3. White Space

Review overall composition.

Where possible:

* increase breathing room
* improve visual balance
* simplify unnecessary density

Luxury design often comes from subtraction rather than addition.

---

### 4. Motion Review

Motion should become nearly invisible.

Movement should feel:

* organic
* calm
* effortless
* premium

Inspired by:

Apple

Nike

Bossa Nova rhythm

Avoid:

* excessive bouncing
* attention-seeking movement
* rapid transitions

---

### 5. Emotional Experience

The homepage should communicate:

Welcome.

Comfort.

Gathering.

Family.

Hospitality.

Memory.

The visitor should feel invited before interacting with the interface.
