Our Story

There was a patient
who changed everything.

She was seven years old. She'd been struggling in school for a year. Teachers thought she was distracted. Her mother thought she wasn't trying.

It turned out she simply couldn't see the board. One exam. One pair of glasses. Her grades changed within a month.

That moment became the foundation of everything Dr. Lara built. Lotilla-Lara Optical Clinic was never meant to be just an eye clinic. It was meant to be the place where people discover what they've been missing.

"Vision is not just about seeing clearly.
It's about seeing yourself — and your world — differently." — Dr. Eldie L. Lara, O.D.

What We Do

Precision care.
For every kind of vision.

Every service is designed around one goal: that you leave seeing better than when you arrived — and understanding exactly why.

Comprehensive Eye Exam

A full diagnostic assessment of your vision and eye health. We check more than your prescription — we check your future.

You leave with a complete picture of your eye health.

Prescription Lenses

Single vision, bifocal, progressive. Zeiss and premium lens technology matched to your lifestyle, not just your chart.

You leave seeing 20/20. Or better.

Frame Selection & Fitting

Curated styles from classic to contemporary. Our fitting process ensures your frames feel like they were made for your face — because they should.

You leave with frames that match how you see yourself.

Pediatric Eye Care

Children's vision affects everything: reading, focus, confidence. We make the exam comfortable, thorough, and clear for the whole family.

You leave knowing your child sees the world they deserve.

Contact Lens Fitting

Trial fitting, hygiene guidance, brand matching. We find the lens that works with your eyes — not against them.

You leave with lens-ready confidence and a complete care plan.

Follow-Up & Monitoring

Vision changes. We track it. Annual recalls, prescription updates, and ongoing care — we remember you between visits.

You leave knowing your vision has someone watching over it.

Your Journey

One appointment.
A lifetime of clarity.

We see you — exactly.
01

You notice something.

Squinting at screens. Headaches after reading. Your child holding books too close. Something tells you it's time. You're right.

02

You reach out.

A WhatsApp message. A call. One of our team responds within the hour. You get a real time slot — not a waiting list.

03

The morning of your visit.

No sterile waiting room energy. The clinic feels like it was designed for calm. Dr. Lara will see you — not a rushed substitute.

04

We see you — exactly.

A full diagnostic exam. We explain every reading, every number, every implication. You understand your own vision by the time we're done.

05

You try on a pair of frames.

And you see yourself differently. That moment — when the frame fits your face and your life — is what we come to work for.

06

The first morning with your glasses.

You look out a window. The world is sharp. The things you'd stopped noticing — a leaf, a face, a word on a sign — are suddenly there again.

07

We stay with you.

An annual recall. A prescription check. A message if something feels off. Your vision changes over a lifetime. So does our care.
